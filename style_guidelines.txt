Overall look and feel of SportsMate

Colours
----------
We want to use a vibrant color theme. This is because we want to emulate the excitement associated with sports.

- #1CC283
- #14DE40
- #2EB29E
- #63C2BE
- #9FE3E2

Iconogrophy
----------
Icons will be simple and minimalistic. They will have slight shading to give the feel of them being 3D despite htem being 2D.

Typogrophy
----------
We will be using Montserrat from Google Fonts

Links
----------
Links will not defaultly be underlined but they will be blue. They will become underlined when hovered over.

Buttons
----------
The buttons will not have borders. The main sighup button will be rounded but the other buttons will be squares.
